The X-Files

Or: When Fox still knew how to make good shows.

First airing in September of 1993, the X-files stars David Duchovny as the fearless and witty FBI agent Fox "Spooky" Mulder and Gillian Anderson as the rational and focused Dr. Dana Scully. The show follows their work on the titular X-Files, a department within the FBI that investigates paranormal activity. Ever the skeptic, Scully calls Mulder's beliefs into question yet time and time again she finds that she must confront her own conundrum: science vs. superstition, the logical vs. the unexplainable. More often than not, the pair find themselves unable to fully explain the phenomena they encounter. Government conspiracies, military coverups, alien abductions and more await the pair of FBI agents. One thing is for certain: the truth is out there.""
